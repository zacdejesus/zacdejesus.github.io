Privacy Policy

Fee Calculator · Last updated: June 8, 2026

Fee Calculator (“the app”) is provided by Alejandro De Jesus. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data we collect

The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email, location, or contacts. The fee amounts you type are processed on your device and are not sent to us.

Advertising

The app displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google’s SDK may collect and process certain information, including a device advertising identifier (IDFA), app interaction data, and approximate, non-precise technical data. On iOS, personalized advertising is only used if you grant permission through Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt; otherwise non-personalized ads are shown. Learn more: Google partner sites policy.

Your choices

iOS: change tracking permission anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. You can reset or limit your advertising identifier in your device settings.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.
